The contents of this folder are starting material to initalize the same simulations performed in this work. 
Requests for the raw data can be made to the associated contact on the publication. 
Reduced versions of the MD related data are being managed to be accessible 1-1-2021. 

VMD, Matlab (version 2020+), NAMD, the tRAMD extension of this software, and the tRAMD (run time material from https://www.h-its.org/downloads/ramd/) 
  are prerequists for functionality.
